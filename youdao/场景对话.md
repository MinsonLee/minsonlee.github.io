1. Make A Phone Call
- 介绍自己：Hello xxx,this is Minson.
- 介绍自己：Hi xxx, it's Minson.
- 介绍并询问：Hi,this is Minson. Is xxx there?
- 很高兴你在：I'm glad you are in.
- 介绍通话目的：I'm calling you to ask xxx(if you are busy next week?).
- 空了回拨给我：Call me when you are free.
- 跟你通话很开心，再见！Nice taking to you, Bye.
- 我更早之前打过给你，但没接通。I tried to call you earlier, but I can't get through.

2. 道别
- See you later,Minson. 等会见
- I have to go now. 我必须先离开/下线了.

3. 感谢
- Thank you so much. 非常感谢你
- Thanks for helping me. 感谢你帮助我
- Thank you.It is kind of to you give me the number. 谢谢你给我你的号码


4. 道歉
- Sorry for being late. 为我的迟到感到抱歉
- I want to say sorry for the mistake. 我想为我的错误说一句抱歉
- I'm sorry that xxx(接句子，如：I can't come to your patty).
- I can't come to your patty ==so== I'm very sorry about that.
- I want to apologize in advance for the lights.（I want to apologize for xxx.）为xx道歉
- I'm sorry for breaking your computer. That's okay.Don't worry about it.

5. 询问排期
- Are you free on Monday? No, I'm out of the office all day./Yes, I don't have any plans all day.
- Can you do Friday afternoon?  Yes, I'm meeting a friend in the morning, but i have time after lunch.
