- take a rain check （婉拒邀请，表示：这次可能不行，下次改天我请客）
> - Do you want to take a rain check?
> - I'm sorry, but I'll have to take a rain check for dinner this Saturday. Would next weekend work for you?
> - I'm sorry, Mimi, I'm just too exhausted to go out tonight. Could I take a rain check?  Ok, When you are free just give me a call.

- flight departs at xxx ：飞机起飞时间（flight leaves-Where do you want to leave from?）
> - What time does your plane depart?
> - My flight departs early in the morning.

- flight lands at xxx：飞机着陆时间
> - When is your plane land in England?
> - My flight lands in England at six-fifty p.m.
