### 3. 询问价格**

**到伦敦的费用多少？**
>- How much is the fare to London? 到伦敦的费用多少？
>- What is the fare to London? 到伦敦的票价多少？
>   - fare 费用(一般指：车费、船费、飞机票价等费用)、运费
>- How much does it cost to get to London ? 到伦敦需要花费多少钱？
>- What's the price of a ticket to London ? 到伦敦的票价格是多少？
>   - price of something xxx的价格
>- How much do I pay to go to London ? 我需要支付多少钱去到伦敦？
>
> It's one hundred fifty US dollar. 需要 150 美元



### 4. 询问搭乘细节

**到伦敦，我该坐那趟车？**
>- Which train should I take to London ? 到伦敦我该坐哪趟车？
>- Which train goes to London ? 哪趟车到伦敦？
>- Which train stops at London ? 哪趟车在伦敦停？【终点站在伦敦】
>- Which train is going toward London ? 哪趟车开往伦敦？


**我该在哪站换乘？**
>- Where am I supposed to transfer ? 我应该在哪站换乘？[be supposed to ...]应该做
>- Where am I suppoosed to change ? 
>- Where do I change trains ?
>- Where should I change trains ?
>- At whcih station should I change?

**你可以在体育西路换乘3号线**
>- You can change to the Line 3 at TiYuXi Rode.
